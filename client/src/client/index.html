How to Play

Privacy Note: Motion.io uses a model that runs entirely on your device to track hand movements. All processing is done in your browser: no video is ever recorded or sent to a server.
  • MOVEOpen hand away from center
  • SPRINTPinch to move faster
  • ATTACKFist to shoot orb along its orbit
  • POINTPoint for normal movement
  • RECENTERPress C to reset center
