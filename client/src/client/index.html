How to Play

Privacy Note: Motion.io uses a model that runs entirely on your device to track hand movements. All processing is done right here in your browser: no video is ever recorded or sent to a server.
  • MOVE Move open hand away from center
  • RETRACT Close/Pinch hand to pull flail
  • ATTACK Point finger to throw flail
  • RECENTER Press 'C' to reset center point
